# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors should keep technical discussion direct, evidence-based, and respectful. Disagreement about benchmark design is expected; personal attacks, harassment, and hostile conduct are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Be precise about evidence and assumptions.
- Separate criticism of artifacts or methods from criticism of people.
- Prefer reproducible examples over rhetorical claims.
- Respect data-use, privacy, and publication constraints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Doxxing or disclosure of private information
- Misrepresentation of benchmark results or hidden assets
- Intentionally compromising blind evaluation conditions

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may remove content or contributions that violate these expectations.
